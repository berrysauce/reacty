Let's talk numbers...

reacty is free

Currently, there are no paid plans for reacty. Even if paid plans come in the future, all current features will be included for free.

reacty is...

 privacy friendly

 easy to implement

 modern

 responsive (works on mobile and desktop)

 So, why is reacty free? Surely the customer is the good, and you're making money from selling customer data, right?

No. I can offer reacty for free since I don't have a lot of costs. Currently, I only pay for the domain. The service is hosted serverlessly (for free, on deta.sh) and the databases are free as well (also on deta.sh).

 Ok, but what about user data? What do you store?

reacty was made with privacy and security in mind. Passwords are hashed and stored on an encrypted database, and user data is kept at a minimum. I only store login info (not even your email) and user preferences.

 And what about my website visitors? Will they be tracked?

Nope. If a user fills out the widget (aka. clicks close or submits feedback) a variable will be stored in the browser's local storage, so the widget won't be shown twice. After submitting feedback, the counter for the selected reaction will increase. I do not store which user visited which website, where they came from, and so on.

Feedback, simplified.

feeed is a simple to implement widget for your website. Its purpose is to ask for feedback, not to track your visitors through the web.



You don't need to be a pro.

Just give it a try and you'll see how easy to use feeed is!

implement feeed with one line

You don't need to know how to code. Just insert one line of code feeed generates for you.

feeed doesn't know you

To use feeed, you don't have to give it your email address or set a password. Just save the link.

feeed is tiny

Since feeed doesn't require a ton of code to run on your website, it won't slow it down.
